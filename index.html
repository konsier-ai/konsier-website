5. Why This Structure Works

Function, Not Title

VA-delegation experts agree: delegation works when tasks are grouped by function with predictable outputs — not by job title. These JDs strip out builder + automated tasks so each VA owns clean workflows. The known anti-pattern (mixed-function role lists) is avoided.

Documentation as Byproduct

Every n8n workflow has sticky notes. Every AI agent has a versioned system prompt. By launch, the training SOPs for VAs are 80% written — just polished into Notion guides. Experts: invest 2-3 days in SOPs up front → VA independent in week 1 vs 3-4 weeks.

System First, Team After

Liam Ottley (AAA): consistent lead generation must precede team hiring — the #1 agency failure is hiring too early. Role 2 builds the system; the VAs are hired only once it produces steady output. The VAs depend on the architecture existing first.
